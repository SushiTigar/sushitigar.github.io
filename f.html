CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT VIDEO

Interview Video

This video features several candid interviews with Nordstrom Employees discussing the benefits of Continuous Improvement strategies on their workflow. These interviews were shot over the course one full day. The video was featured at a large internal recognition meeting.

My Contributions
I was involved with staging, lighting, and recording audio during the video shoot. I was the sole editor during post-production.

Tools Used
A Canon 6D was used to record the video and a Zoom H4N to record the audio. I used Adobe Premiere to edit the video and Adobe Audition to edit the audio.

Wire Fraud Awareness

eLearning Course

This course helps learners understand Wire Fraud and avoid losing thousands of dollars when they go to close on a home.

My Contributions
I created 100% of this course with consultation of legal and IT experts. The content is based on the best practices the FBI and other security agencies promote. I designed the course graphics and developed the eLearning.

Tools Used
Piktochart and Articulate Rise were the primary tools used to develop this course. The icons were sourced from Piktochart but I created the layout, color, and design. I imported the finished graphics into Articulate Rise and customized it to make the course presentation seamlessly illustrated.

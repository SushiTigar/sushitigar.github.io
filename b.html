RETAIL MATH

eLearning Course

This course covers the financial topics pertaining to the Retail industry and teaches learners how to calculate important figures such Gross Margin and Markdown Percentages. The course features voiceovers, animations, video interviews, and interactive math problems. There is even a practice area that generates an infinite number of practice math problems.

My Contributions
I developed roughly 75% of this course on my own. I scripted, recorded, and produced voiceovers; animated graphics within Storyline; created graphics in Adobe Illustrator; wrote some JavaScript to produce math problems dynamically; and developed the course flow in Storyline.

Tools Used
Articulate Storyline is the primary tool used to develop this course. I additionally used Adobe Illustrator, Audition, and Premiere to produce the graphics, audio, and video.
